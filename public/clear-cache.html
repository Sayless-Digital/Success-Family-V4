Having login or loading issues? This will clear your cached data and service worker, then reload the app with a fresh start.

What This Does:

  1. Unregisters the old service worker
  2. Clears all cached data
  3. Clears browser storage
  4. Reloads the app with fresh data

Manual Alternative (Recommended):

  1. Press Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows/Linux) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac) to hard refresh
  2. Or open DevTools (F12)
  3. Go to Application tab
  4. Click "Clear site data"
  5. Reload the page
